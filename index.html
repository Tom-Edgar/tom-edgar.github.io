About

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Colorado State University Pueblo. My interests include combinatorics, number theory, and the visualization of mathematics.

I am especially interested in "proofs without words" and in creating visual explanations that highlight the beauty and surprise of mathematics while hopefully showing what a theorem means and how a formal proof might arise from a diagram.

I also run the YouTube channel Math Visual Proofs, where I create short visual explanations and mathematical animations.
